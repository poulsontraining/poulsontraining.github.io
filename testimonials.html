Testimonials

Linda, 65

I was nervous about training in a gym but knew that I had to regain the strength and fitness I had lost after breaking my leg or lose it forever. A trial session with Vikki gave me the confidence to sign up to a four week programme and now I’m hooked! What I appreciate is her ability to tailor a programme to my specific needs and to challenge me to push myself harder while encouraging every small achievement. When I was away for a couple of months over the summer, she very kindly put together a fitness programme that I could follow without going to the gym. I’m training now for a 100 kilometre walk but more importantly working out with Vikki is helping me to make improving and maintaining my fitness a key element of my lifestyle in retirement. It’s actually quite fun too!

Tracy, 55

My husband gave me three months notice to get ski fit, I am 55 and needed help! Vikki designed me a fantastic programme, based on three sessions a week for 12 weeks, she tailored the session to my ability and gradually increased my fitness in preparation for my holiday. Each session worked on different areas and she made it really fun (never thought I'd say that about gym training). I am now hooked and have told my husband I need a personal trainer all year round if he expects me to ski in my fifties! Thank you Vikki, I am really looking forward to my next ski holiday.

Sam, 27

I had pretty much given up any hope of working out at the gym (any excuse eh) until I met Vikki. She wrote an entire training plan for me, modifying traditional exercises and adding in new ones to avoid any strain on my elbow. She is great at thinking outside the box and is continually giving me new things to try. Can't thank her enough.

Marion, 75

I’m a writer, busy and active, and before I retired I had a full time job, children, then grandchildren, so where do you fit in the time to exercise? Now my body tells me - I definitely had the balance wrong! I have a lot of arthritis and sciatica from a couple of slipped discs. Things I used to be able to do easily are now painful, so of course I avoid doing them - and then my muscles weaken, and complain even more if I do try to use them. Vikki is helping me to work around all that, and to gradually get these weak muscles working better. I’ve been going to a pilates class for a few years and found it very helpful, but my particular body-issues kept getting in the way. Vikki's one-to-one guidance makes a fantastic difference. I like how systematic she is - the programme she has designed for me is clearly thought out, working towards each goal in simple stages. She’s encouraging but realistic. She explains what each exercise contributes so I understand why I'm doing it. It’s always at a level where I can feel competent and yet challenged, and she adapts the exercises constantly in response to how she sees them working for me. I can’t change what’s happening to my joints but getting the muscles more active helps take the strain off them. I’ve known this for years, but having Vikki’s focused guidance has motivated me to practise in-between sessions, because I can already feel that it’s helping.

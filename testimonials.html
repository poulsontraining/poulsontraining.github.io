Marion, 75

I’m a writer, busy and active, and before I retired I had a full time job, children, then grandchildren, so where do you fit in the time to exercise? Now my body tells me - I definitely had the balance wrong! I have a lot of arthritis and sciatica from a couple of slipped discs. Things I used to be able to do easily are now painful, so of course I avoid doing them - and then my muscles weaken, and complain even more if I do try to use them. Vikki is helping me to work around all that, and to gradually get these weak muscles working better. I’ve been going to a pilates class for a few years and found it very helpful, but my particular body-issues kept getting in the way. Vikki's one-to-one guidance makes a fantastic difference. I like how systematic she is - the programme she has designed for me is clearly thought out, working towards each goal in simple stages. She’s encouraging but realistic. She explains what each exercise contributes so I understand why I'm doing it. It’s always at a level where I can feel competent and yet challenged, and she adapts the exercises constantly in response to how she sees them working for me. I can’t change what’s happening to my joints but getting the muscles more active helps take the strain off them. I’ve known this for years, but having Vikki’s focused guidance has motivated me to practise in-between sessions, because I can already feel that it’s helping.

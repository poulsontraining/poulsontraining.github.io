Personal Training


We all live incredibly busy lives and it’s easy to forget to prioritise ourselves. I’m here to help you understand where your fitness is now, where you want to be and support you in getting there the best way possible.

I focus on improving your overall health and wellbeing, teaching you movements and exercises that incorporate multiple muscle groups in order to improve the following:

  • Strength and power
  • Balance and co-ordination
  • Range of motion and flexibility
  • Aerobic and anaerobic fitness
  • Confidence and mental attitude

Leading to you being able to live everyday life easier.

We will work together to determine short, medium and long term goals that we will track over time in a manner that suits your individual needs. My aim is to help you reach your goals by focusing on progress not perfection, learning to become consistent so exercise is a part of your life.

On signing up we will have a consultation where we get to know each other. I will then tailor a programme to your specific needs which we will use in training sessions and reassess periodically. We will work together to build a personalised training programme that excites you and leaves you looking forward to the next session.

I work with a wide range of clients of all ages with different goals in mind, so please get in touch to discuss any requirements you have or to find out more. Whether you are brand new to exercise or a regular at the gym we can design a training programme that works best for you.

I am also Exercise Referral qualified, this means I am trained to tailor programmes for people with medical conditions including:

  • Hypertension, Hypercholesterolemia
  • Asthma, COPD
  • Osteoarthritis, Rheumatoid Arthritis, Joint Replacement, simple mechanical back pain, Osteoporosis
  • Depression, Stress, Anxiety
  • Diabetes Type 1 and Type 2, Obesity

I am REPs registered and insured and have the following qualifications:

REPs Qualifications:

  • Level 3 Personal Trainer
  • Exercise Referral
  • Sports Conditioning
  • Outdoor Exercise
  • Circuits Instructor
  • Spin Instructor

Other Qualifications:

  • SUF Cycle Instructor
  • Stages Trained
  • Zumba Instructor
  • Mental Health Awareness for Sport and Physical Health Trained (Mind)
  • First Aid at Work (British Red Cross)

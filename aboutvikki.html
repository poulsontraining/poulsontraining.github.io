About Vikki


Me in a nutshell...

- I love being outdoors be that walking, hiking, kayaking or swimming

- Weather permitting I spend my weekends travelling around the country with my husband in our self converted camper van Vinnie

- I’m a big fan of all animals, although I currently don’t have a pet of my own I will happily steal a cuddle from yours!

- I like spending time with family and friends eating, playing games and watching movies

- My most annoying habits are always singing the wrong words to songs with confidence and always falling asleep by 10pm even on special occasions (one day I’ll make it to midnight on new years eve!)

- I’m passionate about supporting people suffering from pain or injury but motivated to safely get their health and fitness back on track

After five years I decided to have a break from London and headed off to travel around Asia and Europe with my husband. It was during this year that I started to realise how big a role exercise had played throughout my life in keeping my stress levels down and energy up when work and life had felt like it was getting on top of me.

I decided it was time to educate myself on a broader range of exercises and the relationship they have with our bodies and to help others see the benefits all types of activities can have on our day to day lives.

On my return to the UK I retrained to become a Level 2 Fitness Instructor and Level 3 Personal Trainer and joined a large health and fitness club to gain as much experience as I could teaching group fitness classes, group PT and 1 to 1 PT.

I now work with clients in the gym, at their homes or virtually with the aim of providing people with the knowledge, motivation and understanding of how best exercise can transform all aspects of their lives.

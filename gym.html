Gym - Snap Fitness Wallington

Snap Fitness Wallington is currently closed due to the COVID-19 outbreak. All personal training is being done virtually.

I work from a Gym on Wallington high street called Snap Fitness Wallington. It is a friendly, small commercial gym with a great community. It has top of the range cardio equipment, studio, resistance machines and large free weights area with 2 squat racks and a deadlift platform. My clients do not have to be members of the gym to train with me, however are welcome to become members at a discounted rate if they wish.
